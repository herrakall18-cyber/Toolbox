Why use a cups to grams converter?

Baking by volume (cups) can be wildly inaccurate because ingredients settle, pack, or aerate differently. Scooping flour can give you anywhere from 110 g to 150 g per cup. This cups to grams converter uses standard baker’s densities so you get reliable weight every time — whether you're measuring all‑purpose flour, granulated sugar, or butter. It’s the simplest baking conversion calculator for switching between volume and weight.

Grams to cups conversion made simple

Need the reverse? Click the swap button (⇄) to use the grams to cups converter. Just type in grams, pick your ingredient, and the tool instantly tells you how many cups you need. No more hunting for a flour grams to cups chart — this does the math for you. Perfect for adapting recipes from European or American sources.
