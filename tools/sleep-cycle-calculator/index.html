Wake up refreshed: the science of sleep cycles

Waking up in the middle of a deep sleep stage often leaves you groggy and disoriented. Our sleep cycle calculator (also known as a bedtime calculator) uses the average 90‑minute cycle to help you plan your bedtime. By aligning your alarm with the end of a cycle — when sleep is lightest — you’ll feel more refreshed. Enter your desired wake‑up time and typical minutes to fall asleep, and the tool will show you the best moments to go to bed.

Most adults need 5–6 full cycles (7.5 to 9 hours). The calculator gives you several options, from a “recharge” nap of 3 cycles to a full night of 6 cycles. If you’ve ever asked “what time to go to bed” to avoid that heavy-headed feeling, this wake up refreshed calculator takes the guesswork out. Use it as your daily sleep time calculator for consistent, energetic mornings.

For best results, keep a consistent wake‑up time (even on weekends) and reduce screen light before bed. Your body’s natural rhythm works with the 90‑minute pattern — let our sleep cycle calculator help you capture it.
