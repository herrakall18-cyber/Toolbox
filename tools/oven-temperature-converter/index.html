Why use an oven temperature converter?

Recipes from different countries often use Fahrenheit, Celsius, or gas marks — and getting it wrong can ruin a dish. Our oven temperature converter gives you accurate conversions instantly. Whether you need a precise fahrenheit to celsius oven conversion for a delicate pastry or a quick gas mark to celsius lookup for a Sunday roast, this tool handles it in one click.

Many vintage European cookbooks still rely on gas marks (1 to 10), while modern ovens in North America default to Fahrenheit. A common conversion: 350°F equals about 177°C, which is Gas Mark 4. Memorising every equivalent is tedious — this converter does the work for you, so you can focus on cooking, not calculating.

Whether you're baking bread, roasting vegetables, or following a family recipe from overseas, reliable oven temperature conversion ensures consistent results. Bookmark this page for your next international cooking adventure.
