How to use this monthly car payment calculator

Estimate your auto loan payment instantly. Enter the car loan amount (vehicle price minus down payment), your annual interest rate, and the loan term in months. This car loan calculator gives you the monthly payment, total interest paid, and the full amortization schedule. It’s perfect for comparing financing offers or planning your budget before you visit the dealership.

Our auto loan payment calculator uses the standard amortization formula (fully amortizing loan). Each month, part of your payment goes toward interest and the rest reduces the principal. The schedule shows exactly how your loan balance declines over time. Use it to see how extra down payment or a shorter term reduces total interest — a smart move for any car buyer.

Whether you’re shopping for a new or used vehicle, this monthly car payment calculator helps you make confident decisions. No backend, no page reload — just fast, accurate results. Bookmark it for later.
